Safeguarding & Policies

The safety and wellbeing of children, young people and adults at risk is everyone's responsibility, and a priority in everything we do.

Our commitment

At Loughborough United Reformed Church, the safety and well-being of children, young people, and adults at risk are our top priorities. We are committed to creating a safe and welcoming environment for everyone. If you have any concerns or need support, please reach out to the appropriate contact below — your safety and well-being matter to us.

In an emergency, or if a child or adult is in immediate danger, always call 999.
